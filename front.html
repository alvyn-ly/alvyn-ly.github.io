This is the work I've done. It might not be a huge amount of work yet, but its still growing!

What you will find here is a collection of work that I have done in my academic, professional, and personal career. Some of the stuff you will find here will range back from my early Computer Science courses in San jose State University, to current musings and hobbies that stretch up til today.

What you'll find here is a collection of my most recent works.

  • DoT Leafet - Department of Transportation All-in-One application that takes everything great about leaflet and its plugins, customizes it for DoT usage and Salesforce integration, and gives it to the Development team as a clean n' easy, heavily customizable application for use across all city sites.
  • Let it Ryde - Built using a MEAN stack, Let it Ryde takes what a ride sharing service provides for its users and attempts to fully imitate all of its major functions. While an imitation of Uber/Lyft, Let it Ryde successfully integrates the full lifecycle of login/signup, ride request, locational tracking, service reviewing, real time user/driver coordination, and more to both sides of the user and driver service interaction.
  • Wump - Given how powerful of a platform Unity is to develop on, Wump is the prpduct of an attempt at making a fully fledged, start to finish game with puzzle concepts and a focus on fine motor skills. Developed in C#, Wump takes physics concepts, flashing lights, and thumping music to the next level!... sort of.
